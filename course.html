The Foundation

Why Master
Conflict Resolution?

This course equips NGO professionals with essential conflict resolution, negotiation, and mediation skills to handle disputes effectively and build stronger communities.

What it is

Conflict resolution is the strategic process of resolving a dispute by providing for at least some of each side's needs and addressing their interests.

Why NGOs Need It

In high-stakes environments and team safety and project success is depend on the ability to navigate local tensions and cultural sensitivities.

Community Impact

Conflict Types
